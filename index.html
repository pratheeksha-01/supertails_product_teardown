The Core Problem: Trust

Delivery Anxiety

Promised

60 minutes.

Actual

Days later. No updates.

This isn't just a bad experience — it's the ultimate trust killer for pet parents who run out of food.

The Result

Users open Blinkit or Zepto instead.

Delivery failure resets the loyalty clock.

My Proposed Solution

Feature: Delivery Promise Score

Show a real-time reliability signal at checkout based on stock and courier SLA data.

  • Real-Time Calculation

    Dynamic calculation based on user pin code, local warehouse stock, and courier metrics.

  • Auto-Protection Protocol

    If score < 90%, auto-apply a 5% wallet credit explicitly communicating potential delays.

Goal:

Shift narrative from "They failed" to "They compensated me."
